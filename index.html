Rock-N-Rona: The Throwdown

Choose your contender!

Thee Duck

Thee Duck, a hybrid duck-troll, uses quick wit and flat jokes to distract his enemies and deliver powerful body-blows. A chronic neck ailment leaves his defenses weak.

The Buccalo

The Buccalo, after willfully transfusing his blood for buffalo sauce, The Buccalo saw insane stamina and unrivaled attack strength. However, a dependency on Jack Daniels and Coca-Cola left him merely exceptional and not legendary.

Duke

Duke, an enhanced cyborg human, takes advantage of a built-in and always on Heads Up Display to augment his reading abilities. His attacks are average in strength, but nearly never miss.

MaDuke

MaDuke, aka 'The Wall'. After a tragic chemical accident left him nearly unbreakable, he developed a deep understanding of stoicism. His attacks are powerful but slow. His defense is unmatched.

Hakk

Hakk, half ostrich and retired WNBA Super-Star, uses his length to keep opponents at bay while launching vicious, but often inaccurate headbutts thanks to his incredible neck-length.
